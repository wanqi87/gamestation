About the Game

Cubes 2048.io takes the classic 2048 puzzle game to a whole new dimension. In this innovative 3D multiplayer version, you'll compete with players from around the world as you merge cubes, create larger numbers, and aim for the highest score possible.

How to Play

  • Move your cube around the 3D space to collect and merge similar numbers
  • Combine identical numbers to create larger values
  • Aim to reach 2048 while competing with other players
  • Watch out for opponents and strategic positioning
